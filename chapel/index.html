--- layout: base title: The Day Chapel ---

The Day Chapel is located in the State Botanical Garden of Georgia at the University of Georgia. Nestled in the trees of the garden, we feel that the Day Chapel will lend itself to a private, serene wedding and reception, in a natural setting.

The Day Chapel is located in Athens, GA at 2450 S. Milledge Ave , which is about 1 mile south of Highway 10 (i.e., "the loop"). You can use the map below to get directions to the gift shop at the Botanical Gardens. Just follow the Google Map directions into the garden gates, then follow the signs to the Day Chapel.
